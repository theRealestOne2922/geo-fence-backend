GPS Verification

To decrypt a geo-fenced file, your physical GPS position will be compared against the sender's authorized origin using precise mathematical distance calculation.

1
GPS Capture — Your browser reads your live latitude and longitude via the Geolocation API.
2
Haversine Check — The server calculates the great-circle distance between you and the origin in meters.
3
Geo-Fence Gate — If you are within 65 meters, the RSA container is unlocked and your AES key is released.
4
Decrypt & Download — The AES-256 key decrypts the file and it downloads to your device automatically.
